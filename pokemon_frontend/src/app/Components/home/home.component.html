Equip yourself with essential gadgets:

  • Poke Balls: Your trusty companions for catching and storing Pokemon.
  • Pokedex: An electronic encyclopedia that records data about the Pokemon you encounter.
  • Incubators: Used to hatch Pokemon Eggs and expand your collection.
  • Potion and Revive: Heal and revive your Pokemon during battles and adventures.
  • Poke Coins: The in-game currency for acquiring items and enhancing your Pokemon journey.
